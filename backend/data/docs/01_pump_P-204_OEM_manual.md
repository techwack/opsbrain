# OEM Maintenance Manual — Centrifugal Pump P-204
Manufacturer: Kirloskar Brothers Ltd. | Model: KDS-150/32 | Service: Crude transfer, Unit-2

## 1. Operating Envelope
- Rated flow: 150 m³/h at 32 m head
- Suction pressure: 2.5–4.0 bar(g). Operation below 2.0 bar(g) risks cavitation.
- Bearing temperature alarm: 85 °C (high), 95 °C (trip).
- Mechanical seal: John Crane Type 2100, balanced, single cartridge.

## 2. Mechanical Seal (Type 2100)
The mechanical seal is the most failure-prone component. Seal flush plan is API Plan 11
(recirculation from discharge to seal chamber). Seal failure typically presents as:
- Visible product leak at the gland (> 5 drops/min).
- Seal chamber temperature rise above 70 °C.
- Loss of flush flow.

Recommended seal replacement interval: every 8,000 running hours OR on leak detection,
whichever is earlier. Always replace the seal faces and O-rings as a set. Torque the
gland nuts to 25 N·m in a star pattern.

## 3. Bearing Maintenance
- Lubricant: ISO VG 68 mineral oil. Change every 4,000 hours.
- Vibration limit (overall): 4.5 mm/s RMS per ISO 10816-3. Above 7.1 mm/s = shutdown.
- A rising vibration trend with bearing temperature is the classic early indicator of
  bearing wear or misalignment.

## 4. Common Failure Modes
| Failure mode        | Primary cause                  | Early indicator                    |
|---------------------|--------------------------------|------------------------------------|
| Seal failure        | Dry running / loss of flush    | Gland leak, seal chamber temp rise |
| Cavitation          | Low suction pressure           | Crackling noise, flow fluctuation  |
| Bearing failure     | Misalignment, lube degradation | Vibration + bearing temp rise      |
| Impeller erosion    | Solids in crude               | Gradual head/flow loss             |

## 5. Lock-Out Tag-Out (LOTO)
Before any intervention on P-204, isolate suction valve V-204A and discharge valve
V-204B, de-energize motor M-204 at MCC-2, and apply LOTO. A valid Permit-to-Work (PTW)
is mandatory for any seal or bearing job (hot work / confined space rules may apply).
